PROCESSING ACTIVITY #[number]: Internal Administrative Notifications for New Members

1. NAME OF PROCESSING ACTIVITY
   Internal administrative notifications via Discord platform

2. DESCRIPTION AND PURPOSE OF PROCESSING
   Automated notification of authorized union administrators about new membership applications for timely review and approval. Notifications enable efficient management of the new member admission process.

3. LEGAL BASIS FOR PROCESSING
   - Article 12, paragraph 1, item 6) of the Personal Data Protection Act (legitimate interests of the union for efficient membership management)
   - Article 17, paragraph 2, item 4) of the Act (processing by a trade union in the course of its legitimate activities)
   - Equivalent: Art. 6(1)(f) and Art. 9(2)(d) GDPR

4. CATEGORIES OF DATA SUBJECTS
   - Membership candidates (persons submitting applications)
   - Prospective members who have submitted membership requests

5. CATEGORIES OF PERSONAL DATA
   Basic identification data:
   - Full name
   - Email address
   - Quicklook ID (QLID)
   
   Employment data:
   - City (work location)
   - Division
   - Team
   
   Membership data:
   - Anonymity status (visible/anonymous)
   - Application submission date
   - Registration time

6. CATEGORIES OF RECIPIENTS
   Internal recipients:
   - Union President
   - Authorized union administrators
   - Executive board members (as needed)
   
   External processors:
   - Discord Inc. (San Francisco, CA, USA) - for message transport only

7. TRANSFERS TO THIRD COUNTRIES
   YES - Discord Inc. has servers in the United States of America
   
   Legal basis for transfer:
   - Standard Contractual Clauses (SCC) under GDPR
   - Discord Terms of Service and Privacy Policy (serves as DPA)
   
   Safeguards:
   - HTTPS encryption in transit
   - Discord is a GDPR compliant platform
   - Webhook URL protected as environment variable
   - Discord server access restricted with 2FA authentication

8. DATA RETENTION PERIODS
   
   On Discord platform:
   - Messages are stored in Discord channel history according to Discord retention policy
   - Typically 30-90 days depending on server settings
   - Administrators can manually delete messages
   
   In union system:
   - N/A (notifications are transient and not stored after sending)
   - Original data is stored in database according to main ROPA record for membership

9. DESCRIPTION OF TECHNICAL AND ORGANIZATIONAL SECURITY MEASURES
   
   Technical measures:
   - Discord webhook URL stored as encrypted environment variable
   - HTTPS protocol for all communications with Discord API
   - Webhook notifications sent to private Discord channel
   - Rate limiting at API level (protection against abuse)
   - Error handling that prevents data leakage in logs
   
   Organizational measures:
   - Discord server access restricted to authorized administrators
   - Mandatory multi-factor authentication (2FA) for all administrators
   - Discord channel is private (not publicly accessible)
   - Regular review of administrator access list
   - Administrator education on data protection
   
   Personnel measures:
   - Confidentiality agreements with all administrators
   - Clearly defined roles and responsibilities
   - Least-privilege principle (minimal necessary access)

10. LEGITIMATE INTERESTS (IF APPLICABLE)
    
    Legitimate interest of the union:
    - Efficient and timely management of new member admission process
    - Quick response to new applications to protect candidate rights
    - Transparent tracking of membership process
    
    Balancing of interests:
    - Candidate interest: Quick processing of application and membership admission
    - Union interest: Efficient administration and protection of member rights
    - No negative impact on candidate rights (data is not public)
    
    Necessity test:
    - Discord webhook is proportionate to the goal (fast notifications)
    - No less invasive alternatives with the same efficiency exist
    - Data minimization (only necessary information)
